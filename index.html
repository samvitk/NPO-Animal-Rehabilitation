About Us

Here at the California Association for Rehabilitation and Conservation, our mission is to end the cycle of carnage imposed on our wildlife and the environment. This is done through support from anybody, including you. Any donations or awareness towards our cause and other organization's cause is a step to saving this planet, and its inhabitants. We have a donations page with several awarded and credible associations. Adding on to the donations aspect, there is a page containing our various partners. There will also be programs such as fundraisers or other activities. Additionally, there are resources below to learn more about us, or to contact us for further information or other needs!

Facts

In the United States, the ratio of homeless animals to homeless people is 5:1, with around 70% of cats and 10% of dogs lacking permanent homes. Meanwhile, human activities, including deforestation and fossil fuel combustion, result in an annual production of about 220 billion tonnes of CO2, causing irreversible environmental damage. If this trend continues, experts predict dire consequences such as food scarcity, rising sea levels, and harrowing water shortages.
